## Practical Scenario 1: Supporting Decision Making

The company that hired you wants to launch an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) of a new sales system in 3 weeks. The budget is very small. The development team wants to use a Microservices Architecture, as it is "the architecture used by large companies in the market," therefore it is the best option.

Considering that you are the link between the business and the technology, how can you technically justify the choice of a Layered Monolith for this MVP? What would be the main long-term risk of this choice?
